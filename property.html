Jervis Apartments

1-6 Wolfetone Court, Wolf Tone Street, Dublin, Ireland

(01) 532 0630

reservations@jervisapartments.com

Perfectly located where Dublin's shopping quarter meets the vibrant culture of Temple Bar, these apartments are perfect for your city stay. With our Jervis Street Apartments you will find great value alongside a great central location just minutes from Temple Bar, the Guinness Storehouse, O’Connell Street & more.

Our Guests Love

Our apartments are modern, spacious and come fully furnished, and provide the perfect base for work or play. With every apartment comes the following as standard.

  • Bathroom with shower & bathtub, towels and free toiletries
  • Kitchen with full cooking facilities including oven, fridge-freezer, microwave, kettle, toaster
  • Shared lounge and dining area with flat-screen satellite TV
  • High Speed Wifi and Telephone
  • Baby cot and highchairs available on request
  • Full room clean for stays of 7+ days
  • 24 hour reception, with free luggage storage
