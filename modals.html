Getting here from the airport

Best Option: Take the 747 Bus from Dublin Airport to the City Centre. The 747 Bus stops on Lower O’Connell Street which is 4 minute walk, along Henry Street then onto Jervis Apartments Dublin City.
The bus departs from outside the arrivals hall every 15 minutes. Tickets are €7 per adult and the journey can take between 20 to 30 minutes depending on traffic.
